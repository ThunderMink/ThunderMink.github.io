Electronics Repairs

Examples of PCB-level electronics repairs, automotive module repairs, display repairs and component-level troubleshooting.

Opel Astra 2001 ECU Repair

The ECU uses aluminium bond wires that become brittle over time and crack. The unit was repaired using special aluminium solder flux. The damaged bond wires were replaced with solid tin-plated copper wires.

Garrett Turbo Hella Boost Pressure Control Valve PCB

Aluminium bond wires had cracked. The PCB was repaired using special aluminium solder flux, and the failed bond wires were replaced with solid tin-plated copper wires.
