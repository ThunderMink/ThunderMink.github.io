Product

ESP32 NTC Simulator

Digital temperature measurement and programmable NTC thermistor emulation for controllers, heat pumps, test equipment and sensor simulation.

Features

Reads DS18B20 temperature sensors and outputs simulated NTC resistance values using digitally controlled resistance.

Applications

Heat pump testing, controller development, sensor fault simulation, temperature curve emulation and diagnostic work.

Status

Prototype hardware in development. Documentation and firmware will be published when ready.
